TimesWapArena3D

Controls:

WASD - Move

Mouse - Look around

Left Click - Shoot

Space - Jump (or Ascend in vehicle)

E - Enter/Exit Vehicle

Shift - Descend (in helicopter/airplane)

ESC - Exit pointer lock / Pause


Vehicles:

Tanks, Helicopters, and Airplanes - Enter to shoot from them!


Weapons:

Blue - Pistol | Red - Shotgun | Green - Rifle


Power-ups:

Yellow - Speed Boost | Purple - Shield | Orange - Damage Boost


Buildings:

10-story buildings with windows and ramps - Explore multiple floors!

Destructible buildings - Collect materials when you destroy them!


Building Mode:

B - Toggle Build Mode | 1-7 - Select Blueprint | Left Click - Place


Every 10 seconds, your actions are recorded.
After 30 seconds, a ghost clone will spawn and replay them!
